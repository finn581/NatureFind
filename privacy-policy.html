Park Finder Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Introduction

Park Finder ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Authentication data: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name, email address, and a unique user identifier. We do not store your password.
  • Location data: With your permission, we access your device's location to show nearby parks. Location data is used in real-time and is not stored on our servers.
  • Favorites: Parks you save as favorites are stored in your user profile.
  • Reviews: Reviews you submit (including your display name, rating, and review text) are stored and visible to other users.
  • Reports: If you report a review, your user ID and the report details are stored for moderation purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the app experience (showing nearby parks, saving favorites)
  • To display user-generated reviews to the community
  • To moderate content and enforce community guidelines

Third-Party Services

Park Finder uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and data storage. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • National Park Service API: Park data for national parks. See NPS Privacy Policy.
  • Geoapify: Location data for state and local parks. See Geoapify Privacy Policy.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile tab, which will permanently remove your favorites and authentication data. Reviews you have submitted will remain visible but will no longer be linked to your account.

Children's Privacy

Park Finder does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@parkfinderapp.com.
