FAQ

How do I restore my Pro subscription?

Open the app, go to the Layer Panel, tap any Pro feature, and press "Restore Purchase" on the paywall screen. Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID.

Why don't I see trails or campgrounds?

Trails appear when you zoom in closer to an area. Campgrounds require a closer zoom level as well. If you have a Pro subscription, make sure these layers are toggled on in the Layer Panel.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find NatureFind Pro, and tap Cancel Subscription.

The map isn't loading

Make sure you have an internet connection. NatureFind requires network access to load map tiles and park data. Try closing and reopening the app.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at naturefindapp@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion" and we'll remove your account and all associated data within 48 hours.
