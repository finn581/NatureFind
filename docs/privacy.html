Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

NatureFind ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Location data: When you grant permission, we access your device location to show nearby parks, trails, and campgrounds on the map. Location data is used only on-device for map positioning and drive time calculations. We do not store or transmit your precise location to our servers.

Account information: If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address to create your account. This is stored securely in Firebase Authentication.

User-generated content: Reviews, wildlife sightings (including photos and approximate location), and campground contributions you submit are stored in Firebase Firestore and visible to other users.

Photos: Photos you attach to sightings or reviews are uploaded to Firebase Storage. We do not access your photo library beyond the images you explicitly select.

Purchase data: In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or tracking data
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or sell your data
  • We do not collect your contacts, browsing history, or device identifiers for tracking

Third-Party Services

NatureFind uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and image storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Mapbox — map rendering, routing, and terrain. Mapbox Privacy Policy
  • National Park Service API — public park data from the U.S. government
  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — open-source geographic data for trails, campgrounds, and points of interest

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. User-generated content (reviews, sightings) remains visible to the community. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

NatureFind is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To make a request, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
