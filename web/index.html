Features

Everything the Outdoors Needs

From park discovery to campground booking, NatureFind has you covered before, during, and after your adventure.

🗺️

Interactive Nature Map

Explore parks, trails, campgrounds, and wildlife sightings on a live map. Toggle layers to see exactly what matters to you.

🌤️

Park Condition Scores

A 0–100 daily score for every park based on temperature, wind, weather, and precipitation. Know if it's a Great Day before you go.

🏕️

Campground Details

Real campground data from Recreation.gov — sites, amenities, fees, and a direct booking link. Never show up to a closed campground again.

🥾

Trail Maps & Difficulty

Colored polyline trails with difficulty ratings, surface type, distance, and dog-friendly status — sourced directly from OpenStreetMap.

🦌

Wildlife Sightings

Log and discover wildlife sightings from the community. Bears, birds, elk — see what's been spotted near you in the last 24 hours.

📋

Community Conditions

Real-time trail status reports from other hikers — open, partial, or closed. Wildlife activity, crowding levels, and access notes.

⭐

Park Reviews

Read and write reviews for national parks. Rate your experience, upload photos, and help the community find the best spots.

📅

Seasonal Hints

Context-aware seasonal tips for every park — wildflower season, elk rut, whale migration, desert heat warnings, and more.
