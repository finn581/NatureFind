Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

NatureFind is built for outdoor explorers, not advertisers. We collect only what's needed to make the app work and never sell your personal information.

1. Who We Are

NatureFind ("we", "us", "our") is an iOS application that helps users discover national parks, trails, campgrounds, and wildlife sightings. The app is operated by an independent developer based in the United States.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: support@naturefind.app

2. Information We Collect

Account Information
When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique user identifier and, optionally, a display name or email address. Passwords are never stored by us — authentication is handled entirely by Apple or Google.

Location Data
With your permission, we access your device's GPS coordinates to:

  • Show nearby national parks on the map
  • Calculate drive times to parks
  • Find campgrounds and trails near your current position

Location is accessed only while the app is in use ("When In Use" permission). We do not track location in the background. Location data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to our servers or stored without your explicit action (e.g., when you submit a sighting report).

User-Generated Content
When you voluntarily submit content — including park reviews, wildlife sighting reports, condition reports, or photos — that content is stored in our database and may be visible to other users. It is associated with your account display name.

Photos
With your permission, we access your photo library or camera when you choose to attach a photo to a review or sighting report. Photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage only when you explicitly submit them.

Device & Usage Data
We may receive anonymous crash reports and basic diagnostic data through Expo/React Native's built-in telemetry. This data does not include personally identifiable information and is used solely to improve app stability.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app's core features
  • To display user-generated content (reviews, sightings, condition reports) to the community
  • To authenticate you and maintain your session
  • To respond to support requests
  • To detect and prevent abuse or fraudulent activity

We do not use your information for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it to build marketing profiles.

4. Third-Party Services

NatureFind integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google) — database, authentication, and file storage. Firebase Privacy
  • Sign in with Apple — optional authentication method. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication method. Google Privacy Policy
  • National Park Service API — park data, images, and descriptions. No personal data is shared.
  • Mapbox — map rendering and routing. Mapbox Privacy Policy
  • Open-Meteo — weather data. No personal data is shared.
  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — trail and campground data. No personal data is shared.
  • Recreation.gov (RIDB) — campground data. No personal data is shared.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. User-generated content (reviews, sightings, condition reports) is retained until you delete it or request deletion of your account.

You may delete individual reviews and sighting reports from within the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at support@naturefind.app.

6. Children's Privacy

NatureFind is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Security

We use Firebase's industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access to your data is controlled by Firebase Security Rules that prevent other users from modifying your content.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdrawal of consent — revoke location or photo permissions at any time in iOS Settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@naturefind.app.

9. California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of sale. We do not sell personal information. To submit a CCPA request, contact support@naturefind.app.

10. European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our legal basis for processing your data is your consent (for location and photo access) and legitimate interests (for maintaining the app's core functionality). For GDPR-related requests, contact support@naturefind.app.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. For material changes, we may notify you within the app. Continued use of NatureFind after any change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact:

  • Email: support@naturefind.app
